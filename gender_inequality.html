What is Gender Inequality?

Gender inequality refers to Gender discrimination therefore means the ability to distinguish people based on their sex. This is a social problem that impacts education, employment, politics, healthcare; and all aspects of human social interaction.

Effects of Gender Inequality

The inequality between male and female has severe ramifications for the society. They slow down the development of economies, increase poverty levels, promote cyclical violence and …… hinder the growth of societies. All the mentioned disparities can be seen as the factors that need to be solved in order to create fairness rights.

How Can We Address Gender Inequality?

  1. Encouraging people to give every single person equal chances at education and employment.
  2. Promote women folk into leadership positions
  3. Strengthen measures against discriminations on the basis of gender
  4. Eliminate and replace negative cultural attitudes and perceptions
  5. Organize backing for supplementary groups and movements advocating for women’s rights
